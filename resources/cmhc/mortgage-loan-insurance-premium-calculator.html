Mortgage loan insurance premium calculator

Whether you're a first-time homebuyer or refinancing, mortgage loan insurance may be required. Use our calculator to estimate your premium by entering your desired home price and down payment amount. This will give you a clear understanding of your CMHC insurance costs.

IMPORTANT
CMHC mortgage loan insurance is available for amortization periods of up to 30 years if you are a first-time homebuyer or purchasing a new build.
